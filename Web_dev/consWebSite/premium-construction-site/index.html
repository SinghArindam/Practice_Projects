Our Story

Founded in 2008, Spark Infra Construction Group has evolved from a small local contractor to one of the region's most respected construction firms. With a commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction, we've successfully completed over 250 projects across various sectors.

Our team of 120+ professionals brings together decades of experience and a passion for construction excellence. We believe in building not just structures, but lasting relationships and communities.
